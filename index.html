JINKXS MONSOON

Jinkx Monsoon is a theatrical performer, musician, and drag queen taking the stage by storm. Off stage, she is named Hera Hoffer and grew up in Portland, Oregan. After kicking off her career as the winner of RuPaul's Drag Race season five, Jinkx has since won the show again on an all-winners season, released 3 albums, and has been on broadway twice as "Mama Morton" in Chicago.
